# AIM Pricing & Plans

Version: 1.0  
Last updated: February 2026

## Goal

Define a clear monetization structure for AIM across:
- individual athletes,
- serious premium users,
- coaches and academies.

The model balances:
- low-friction acquisition (free entry),
- strong upgrade incentives (AI depth + premium community),
- scalable B2B revenue (coach base fee + per-seat expansion).

---

## Plan Architecture

### Plan Names
- **Kickoff** (Free)
- **First Touch** (Premium Individual)
- **Dugout** (Coach/Academy)

Name rationale:
- Football-native language
- Clear progression path
- Distinct user intent per tier

---

## Billing Model

## Monthly and Annual
- All paid plans have monthly and annual billing.
- Annual billing provides discounted effective monthly pricing.
- Annual discounts are designed to increase retention and cash flow.

## Trial Policy
- **14-day free trial** for all paid plans.
- Full access during trial.
- At trial end:
  - If payment is added: plan continues.
  - If no payment: account reverts to Kickoff.

## Plan Details

## 1) Kickoff (Free)

Target user: New or casual players validating value.

### Included
- Limited AI coach feedback
  - Suggested cap: 5 AI feedback sessions per week
- Basic avatar selection
  - Starter set only
- Basic drill analysis
  - High-level overview only
  - Detailed components/advanced insights hidden
- Basic community chat
  - Public/free channels only
- Access to selected public challenges

### Not Included
- Advanced coach feedback
- Full avatar customization
- Advanced AI drill diagnostics
- Premium community rooms (coaches/managers/scouts)
- Private premium events

Price: **Free**

---

## 2) First Touch (Premium Individual)

Target user: Individual players actively improving and competing.

### Monthly Pricing
- **$12.99/month**

### Annual Pricing
- **$119.88/year** (effective **$9.99/month**)
- Savings vs monthly billing: **$36/year** (~23%)

### Included
- Advanced AI coach feedback
  - Unlimited interactions
  - More contextual and performance-aware coaching
- Full avatar selection
  - Full customization library
- Advanced drill analysis
  - Latest AI model pipeline
  - Deeper technical insights, trend analysis, and full metric visibility
- Premium community chat
  - Premium channels with higher-quality audience
  - Access to coaches/managers/scouts/serious athletes
- Community event invitations
  - Priority access to premium sessions and events
- Challenges
  - Full challenge participation
  - Optional creation of limited-size personal challenges

### Trial
- 14-day free trial with full First Touch features

## 3) Dugout (Coach/Academy)

Target user: Coaches, private trainers, academies, and teams.

## Pricing Structure
- Base subscription + per-seat scaling
- Seat = active student/player under coach management

### Base Pricing
- **Monthly:** $29.99/month
- **Annual:** $299.88/year (effective $24.99/month)
- Annual savings: **$60/year** (~17%)

### Per-Seat Pricing (Scales by Team Size)
- 1-10 players: included in base
- 11-25 players: +$2.99/seat monthly, +$2.49/seat annual
- 26-50 players: +$2.49/seat monthly, +$1.99/seat annual
- 51-100 players: +$1.99/seat monthly, +$1.49/seat annual
- 100+ players: custom enterprise quote

Note: Final per-seat amount depends on required player volume.

## Included
- Coach CRM
  - Player profile management
  - Grouping, tagging, notes, and status tracking
- Homework assignment
  - Assign drills/tasks to students
  - Due dates and completion tracking
- Unlimited challenge creation
  - No participant limits
  - Internal (team-only) and broader/public challenge modes
- Full team analytics control
  - Team-level dashboards
  - Player comparison and trend visibility
  - Comprehensive performance oversight
- Suggested additional value features
  - Multi-coach seats/roles
  - Report export (PDF/CSV)
  - Parent/guardian read-only access
  - Priority support
  - Early-access feature flags

### Trial
- 14-day free trial (recommended cap: up to 10 players in trial)

## Commercial Strategy Notes

## Upgrade Triggers
- Free users hit meaningful limits quickly (coach responses + analysis depth).
- Premium community access acts as social/status motivator.
- Advanced diagnostics and full breakdowns are core conversion drivers.

## Retention Levers
- Annual discounts
- Recurring challenges/events
- Progress continuity and long-term analytics history

## B2B Expansion Levers
- Per-seat scaling increases revenue with academy growth.
- CRM + homework + team analytics create operational lock-in.
- Multi-coach workflows and exports increase institutional value.

## FAQ (Internal Product/Go-To-Market)

### Why include a free tier?
To maximize top-of-funnel growth and allow users to experience visible value before paying.

### Why 14-day trial?
It is long enough for users/coaches to complete onboarding, run multiple drills, and experience measurable insight quality.

### Why base + per-seat for coaches?
It aligns pricing with value delivered and supports both small coaches and larger academies without forcing one rigid package.

### Can pricing be localized later?
Yes. This v1 is USD baseline and can be adapted to regional purchasing power in later iterations.

## Launch Recommendation (v1)

Ship these exact defaults first:
- Kickoff: free
- First Touch: $12.99 monthly / $119.88 annual
- Dugout: $29.99 monthly / $299.88 annual + per-seat
- 14-day trial for both paid plans

Then review after 60-90 days:
- trial-to-paid conversion,
- monthly churn,
- average seats per Dugout account,
- feature-level usage for upsell tuning.
